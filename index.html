Our mission

ExtraEd is a social network platform that aims to match colleges and universities with prospective students based on academics and extracurricular activities.

What makes us different?

ExtraED will be the dedicated platform that will allow scouts, academic recruiters and students to discover, research and communicate with each other at a click of a button.

Students on our platform can:



• Build compelling profiles that can be viewed by academic recruiters and scouts.

• Create their portfolio; highlight their talents not just their academics.

• Forge personal relationships with recruiters.

• Be seen by colleges, irrespective of geographic location or market.
